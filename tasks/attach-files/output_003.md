# Content Design Iteration 003

## Goal Analysis
The goal is to provide a balanced variation that combines different mindsets while addressing research insights:
- Senders often skim and don't read all details.
- Shift perspective: Focus on the recipient's permanent access rather than the sender's loss of control.
- Maintain technical constraints (10 files, 6 MB total).

## Proposed Variation: Balanced Mindset
This version bridges the need for efficiency (skimming) with the need for transparency (consequences of sending). It uses a "What happens next" perspective to explain the recipient experience.

## Design Principle Alignment: Clarity and Transparency
This iteration meets the Clarity principle by simplifying the explanation of ownership. Instead of focusing on technical "revocation," it uses the concept of "permanent copies," which is a concept familiar to most email users (Build trust with recipients).

By moving the ownership explanation directly under the main heading, we meet the Transparency principle for skimmers: they see the "what and why" immediately before they interact with the file picker. This provides context of use and reduces the risk of error by ensuring the sender knows the action is final from the very start.
